Geonwoo Kim · 김건우

Pronounced “guh-noo” · Preferred name: Patrick

I am a Research Professional at the Becker Friedman Institute at the University of Chicago, where I work on econometric theory with Azeem Shaikh and Kirill Ponomarev.

My research asks how we can learn from high-dimensional, imperfectly measured, and algorithmically-generated data, and how we can conduct valid statistical inference in such settings.

I received a B.A. in Mathematics with Honorsfrom Grinnell College, where I was advised by Jeffrey Blanchard.
