Geonwoo Kim

I go by Patrick.

I am a Research Professional at the Becker Friedman Institute at the University of Chicago, where I work on econometric theory with Azeem Shaikh and collaborators.

My research asks how we can learn from data when the variables we care about are high-dimensional, imperfectly measured, or produced by algorithms. I am especially interested in measurement error, computational econometrics, and economic measurement with AI.

I received a B.A. in Mathematics with Honors from Grinnell College.
